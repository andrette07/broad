Organizations don't struggle from a lack of effort — they struggle when direction becomes unclear and momentum turns reactive. The Broad Strategy was founded to address exactly that.

Strategic Advisory Engagement Model

From Diagnostic Clarity to Execution-Ready Architecture

A structured advisory pathway designed to move organizations from diagnostic clarity to execution-ready strategic architecture.

Complimentary Discovery Session No Cost · 45–60 Minutes

Clarify the Challenge. Determine Strategic Fit.

An initial conversation to understand your organization's presenting tensions, context, and readiness — and to determine the right path forward.

Book a Discovery Session
01 Tier One

Clarity & Alignment Assessment

Typical Timeline: 3 Weeks

For leadership teams facing complexity, competing priorities, or internal misalignment who need objective clarity on what is truly happening and what should be prioritized next.

Includes
  • Focused document review
  • Targeted leadership interviews
  • Internal and external analysis
  • Findings & Direction Deck
  • Focus Now / Focus Later Priorities
  • Quick Wins & Stabilizers

Outcome: Leadership gains shared clarity on organizational realities, emerging priorities, and the most strategic path forward.

Begin the conversation
02 Tier Two · Most Complete

Strategic Roadmap & Momentum Design

Additional 5–8 Weeks Following Tier 1

For organizations requiring a structured strategic roadmap and supporting organizational frameworks to align teams, sequence priorities, and steward execution effectively.

Includes Everything in Tier 1, Plus
  • Strategic roadmap & infrastructure
  • Initiative briefs
  • 12–18 Month Sequencing Timeline
  • Governance & accountability design
  • Leadership operating systems
  • Strategic communications framework
  • Leadership prioritization workshop

Outcome: Leadership receives a strategic blueprint aligned to organizational capacity, constraints, and growth objectives.

Begin the conversation
03 Tier Three

Implementation & Momentum Advisory

12-Month Advisory Window

For organizations seeking continued strategic guidance to preserve alignment, momentum, and strategic coherence during implementation. Biweekly reviews during the first 3 months, followed by strategic checkpoints at months 6, 9, and 12.

Includes
  • Execution condition monitoring
  • Priority & sequencing stabilization
  • Leadership advisory support
  • Strategic review & recalibration guidance

Outcome: Strategy remains coherent, executable, and adaptive as implementation unfolds.
